About me

Hola! I am Gerard Martín Escofet, a predoctoral research assistant at CREi for Professors Victoria Vanasco and Vladimir Asriyan. I hold a Double Bachelor’s Degree in Economics UPF-TSE (2015-2019), a Master 1 in Economics (TSE, 2018-2019), and a MSc Econometrics and Mathematical Economics (LSE, 2019-2020).

I am broadly interested in macroeconomics, with an emphasis on economic growth and (regional) inequality. I have also written some projects on monetary economics, political economy, and experimental economics.
